About SortSnap

Your AI-Powered Image Organization Solution

What is SortSnap?

SortSnap is an intelligent image classification tool designed to help you organize your images. Using advanced machine learning technology, it automatically categorizes your images into three main categories.

Generic Images

Common, everyday photos that you might want to organize separately.

Non-Generic Images

Unique or special photos that deserve special attention.

Documents

Important documents and text-based images for easy access.
